Welcome to Coorg

Places to visit in Coorg:-

  1. Abbey Falls - Stunning waterfall, lush greenery
  2. Raja's Seat - Sunset view, peaceful garden
  3. Mandalpatti - Scenic hills, jeep ride
  4. Golden Temple - Buddhist monastery, spiritual ambiance
  5. Coffee Plantations - Aromatic coffee, guided tours
  6. Nagarhole National Park - Wildlife safari, diverse fauna
  7. Dubare Elephant Camp - Elephant bathing, fun activities
  8. Tadiandamol Peak - Trekking, breathtaking mountain views
  9. Pushpagiri Wildlife Sanctuary - Rare birds, nature trails
  10. Harangi Dam - Peaceful reservoir, scenic beauty
