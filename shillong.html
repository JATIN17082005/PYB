Welcome to Shillong

Places to Visit in Shillong:-

  1. Umiam Lake - Serene reservoir, boating and kayaking
  2. Elephant Falls - Stunning multi-tiered cascading waterfall
  3. Shillong Peak - Highest point, breathtaking city view
  4. Laitlum Canyons - Scenic valley, panoramic landscape view
  5. Ward's Lake - Beautiful lake, lush garden surroundings
  6. Don Bosco Museum - Cultural heritage, North-East tribal exhibits
  7. Police Bazar - Shopping hub, bustling market streets
  8. Mawphlang Sacred Forest - Ancient forest, rich biodiversity preserve
  9. Sweet Falls - Tall, picturesque, hidden waterfall
  10. Cathedral of Mary Help - Stunning architecture, peaceful prayer atmosphere
