Welcome to Kaziranga

Places to Visit in Kaziranga:-

  1. Kaziranga National Park - One-horned rhinos, wildlife safari experience
  2. Agaratoli Range - Birdwatching paradise, scenic forest trails
  3. Bagori Range - Elephant safari, rich biodiversity
  4. Kohora Range - Jeep safari, diverse animal sightings
  5. Panbari Reserve Forest - Rare birds, untouched natural beauty
  6. Diphlu River Lodge - Luxury stay, riverside jungle retreat
  7. Kakochang Waterfalls - Refreshing waterfall, scenic trekking destination
  8. Deopahar Ruins - Ancient temple, historical significance remains
  9. Bhairabkunda - Peaceful confluence, stunning natural landscapes
  10. Tea Gardens - Lush greenery, aromatic Assam tea
